Why Am I Here?

I decided to do the Galvanize Web Dev program after attending some of the Learn to Code sessions. I've enjoyed coding since I was a kid, but (for some reason) I never focused on it until now. Now that we've started the program, I feel lucky that what I'm "supposed to" be doing with my time is what I like to do anyway. It will be even better when I'm getting paid for it.

I'm not much of a designer, but I love logic and fixing things, so I think I might want to do back-end development. I'm looking forward to having more varied and challenging work, and making enough money so I can travel a little while I'm still young.
